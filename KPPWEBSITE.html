HISTORY

KPP Minds Tutorial was founded with the goal of helping students grow academically and gain confidence in learning. Inspired by the shared passion for teaching of Kyla, Princess, and Portia, the tutorial centre was created to provide quality, student-focused education in a supportive environment.
Starting as a small tutorial service, KPP Minds Tutorial gradually expanded to offer both online and on-site tutoring, making learning more accessible to students with different needs. The centre focuses on clear explanations, guided practice, and encouragement to help learners develop strong study habits and critical thinking skills.
Today, KPP Minds Tutorial continues to support students by nurturing young minds, building knowledge, and inspiring a love for learning.
